Roasted Coffee

Roasted coffee is beans that undergo a heating process to high temperatures to reveal their aroma and flavor. The roasting process is a crucial step in coffee preparation, as it is at this moment that the beans acquire their unique taste characteristics. There are several degrees of roasting, from light to dark, each of which affects the final taste of the drink in its own way. Light roasting preserves more acidity and fruity notes, while dark roasting makes the taste richer and more bitter. Proper roasting allows the coffee bean's full potential to be revealed, making the beverage aromatic and enjoyable to taste.
